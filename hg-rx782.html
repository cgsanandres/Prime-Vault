Product Description

The iconic mobile suit that started it all returns with major modern upgrades in Bandai's HGUC 1/144 scale RX-78-2 Gundam (Revive) plastic model kit! Released to commemorate the 35th anniversary of Gunpla, this updated version features completely new molds that give the classic mecha ideal proportions, a sleek profile, and vastly improved mobility. True to the High Grade Universal Century line, the kit utilizes high-precision parts separation and snap-fit technology, requiring no glue or cement for a rewarding build.

A huge highlight of this Revive edition is its enhanced joint engineering, allowing it to execute the iconic poses from the original Mobile Suit Gundam TV series with absolute ease. Whether you are a veteran builder or just getting started with the hobby, this kit delivers a fast, fun, and screen-accurate finish straight out of the box without the absolute need for paint.

Product Features

  • 1/144 Scale (Stands approximately 5 inches / 12.5cm tall when completed)
  • Made of high-quality PS, PE, and durable ABS plastics
  • Based on the legendary first mecha from the original Mobile Suit Gundam series
  • Revived design featuring contemporary proportions and an advanced internal structure
  • Excellent articulation with double-jointed elbows and knees for a massive range of motion

  • Traditional snap-fit assembly with no glue or cement required
