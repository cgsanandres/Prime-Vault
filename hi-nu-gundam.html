Product Description

The ultimate evolution of Amuro Ray's mobile suit takes flight with Bandai's spectacularly detailed RG 1/144 scale RX-93-ν2 Hi-ν Gundam! Featured in the novel Mobile Suit Gundam: Char's Counterattack - Beltorchika's Children, this Real Grade kit delivers a new interpretation of intricate detail and realism. The kit utilizes a advanced snap-fit assembly process, ensuring a stable and rewarding build experience without the absolute need for glue or cement.

This Real Grade release is packed with mechanical engineering highlights, featuring a multi-link gimmick that slides the armor panels in tandem with joint articulation to expose the detailed inner frame. Complete with an advanced S-foil wing binder system that houses its iconic Fin Funnels, this model stands as a definitive masterpiece for any Gunpla collection.
